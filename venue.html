VENUE

Live Events at Muthers Studio

Our fully equipped venue hosts up to 120 people and comes with full backline and front of house provided. 32 channel mixer, subs under the stage, side fills and EV main speakers combine with four onstage monitors, 4x12 cabs with Orange heads, sansamp power amp with ampeg cab and a well maintained Pearl export to provide the best sound in Birmingham, on and off stage.

Our venue regularly plays host to touring and local bands on the weekend evenings but the space can be, and has been, used for a host of purposes not limited to: workshops, presentations, talks, listening parties and video shoots.

ACCESSIBILITY NOTE: Whilst the venue and reception are on the ground floor there are a couple of steps leading into the building and unfortunately there isn't wheelchair access or accessible facilities currently.
