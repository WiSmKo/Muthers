MUTHERS STUDIO

One stop shop for creation, refinement, community and realisation.

Based in Digbeth, the cultral beating heart of Birmingham. Muthers studio is one of the most highly respected arts complexes in the Midlands.

Boasting rehearsal spaces for all levels of proffesionalities, its very own recording studio, over 30 lock-ups, a live music venue and two bars offering alcoholic and barista refreshments and small music essentials shop for sticks, strings, picks, leads etc.

Along with the other business operating within the walls of Muthers, music production, video production and guitar servicing are all covered.

Rehearse.

Muthers Studio offers 5 fully equipped rehearsal rooms bookable by the hour. Amps, mics, PA, drum kit, symbals & snare are provided so all you need is guitars & sticks. Keyboards also available upon request.
