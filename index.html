About us...

Based in Digbeth, the cultral beating heart of Birmingham. Muthers studio is one of the most highly respected arts complexes in the Midlands.

Boasting rehearsal spaces for all levels of proffesionalities, its very own recording studio, over 30 lock-ups, a live music venue and two bars offering alcoholic and barrista refreshments and small music essentials shop for sticks, strings, picks, leads etc.

Along with the other business operating within the walls of Muthers, music production, video production and guitar servicing are all covered.

Muthers Studio is quite simply a one stop shop for creation, refinement and realisation.
