REHEARSAL

Each room comes fully kitted out with well maintained, high-quality PA, amps and drum kit so you can just turn up and play. Microphones, cymbals, keyboards and leads are available upon request at reception completely free of charge and guitar hire is available for a small fee. Our standard rooms (1-3) are fully suitable and equiped for 5/6 piece bands, solo artists or anything inbetween.
If you are a larger group, our 120 cap venue is available to rehearse in, wether you need the space or just looking to get gig-ready, or we have our 'small stage' in room 5.
